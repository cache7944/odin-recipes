Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

You'll be surprised how easy it is to make this top-rated guacamole recipe.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this irresistible guacamole recipe:

  • Avacados
  • Lime
  • Salt
    • A teaspoon of salt
  • Vegetables
    • An onion
    • Roma tomatoes
  • Herbs and spices
    • Fresh cilantro
    • Minced garlic
    • Cayenne pepper

Steps

Now let's learn how to make this guac recipe!

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl;
  2. Mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic;
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper;
  4. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
